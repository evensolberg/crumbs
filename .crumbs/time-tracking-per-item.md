# Time tracking per item

No start-timer/stop-timer concept. Could complement story points with actual elapsed time. E.g. `crumbs start <id>` sets status to in_progress and records a timestamp; `crumbs stop <id>` logs elapsed time to the item body.

[start] 2026-03-08 09:58:26  Cool

[stop]  2026-03-08 09:58:37  11s

[start] 2026-03-08 10:03:13  Cool 2

[stop]  2026-03-08 10:03:26  13s  Cool again
